Frequently Asked Questions

How does KiranaStation ensure the freshness of its products?

We have established partnerships with local farmers and suppliers to source the freshest ingredients directly. Our stringent quality control measures ensure that only the best produce reaches our customers.

What payment methods does KiranaStation accept?
KiranaStation accepts various secure payment methods, including credit/debit cards, net banking, and mobile wallets. Your transactions are encrypted and secured to ensure your peace of mind.

*Note: We also accept Cash On Delivery(COD)!

How does KiranaStation handle returns or exchanges?
We strive to provide the best shopping experience for our customers. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, please contact our customer support team within 30 days. We'll be happy to assist you with returns or exchanges. However, vegetables and fruits must be returned within 10 days.

Can I schedule a delivery for a specific date and time?
Yes, KiranaStation offers the flexibility to schedule your delivery for a specific date and time that is convenient for you. During the checkout process, you can choose your preferred delivery slot.

How can I track my order?
Once your order is confirmed, you will receive a tracking link via email or SMS. You can use the link sent in there to track the status of your delivery in real-time.

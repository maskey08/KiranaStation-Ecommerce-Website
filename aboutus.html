Our Journey from Farm to Table

We started this journey from tiny wooden table having our united dream of sourcing healthy and organic food to our community. Not only sourcing the organic products but also helping our local farmers who are the true experts of producing these organic and freshly hand-picked foods. We directly partnered with our local farmers and started from very small office in a wooden table where we four worked together having vision to bring freshest and healthiest food to our community.

In 2022, we implemented this vision when the world was suffering from pandemic disease COVID-19. At that time not only people were suffering from disease and shortages, but also were deprived financially. Then after, we planned this small start-up of helping local farmers and invested our savings and sold our organic products at reasonable price with top-quality. Our journey took turns when we started to get fame and good reviews from our community and we grew high and more. And then we planned for opening our pop-up store converting from tables to counters, small laptops to cashier tables and tiny baskets to shopping-carts. Finally at the end of 2023, we opened our first pop-up store in Kathmandu and named it "Kirana Station" and our first opening day we were overwhelmed of so many people visiting our very first own pop-up store.

We were only small dreamers who envisioned of doing something anew and helping our local farmers and providing our community with healthiest foods but never imagined we would grow this big. We are very grateful to our loyal customers who supported us since the beginning of our journey when we were just based online and till now.
But our journey and vision doesn't end here, we are expanding more in near future and our aim is to open our franchise globally both e-store and physical store.
